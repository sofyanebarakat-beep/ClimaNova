## WHAT IS TOPICAL AUTHORITY

Topical authority means Google recognizes your site as the most comprehensive,
trustworthy source on a given topic. It is achieved by:
1. Covering every sub-topic related to your niche in depth
2. Interlinking related content in logical clusters
3. Publishing consistently on the same topics
4. Earning backlinks from related authority sites
