Services & Repairs

What types of home repair services do you offer?

We cover plumbing, electrical, carpentry, painting, appliance repair, and home maintenance. Whatever needs fixing, we’re here to help.

Can you handle both small and large repair jobs?

Absolutely! From minor leaks to full room renovations, no job is too small or too big for our dedicated, professional team.

Do you offer emergency repair services?

Yes, we provide fast, reliable emergency services for urgent issues like water leaks or electrical problems anytime you need us.

How soon can you start the repair?

Depending on availability, we often start within 24–48 hours after your booking is confirmed for faster service delivery.

Do you bring your own tools and materials?

Yes, our technicians arrive fully equipped with the right tools and quality materials to complete the job efficiently.in 24–48 hours after your booking is confirmed for faster service delivery.

Services & Pricing & Estimates

How do you determine the cost of a repair?

We evaluate the issue, the materials required, and the estimated labor time. Once inspected, we provide a clear, upfront estimate with no hidden surprises.

Do you offer free estimates?

Yes, we provide free basic estimates for most standard repair requests. For complex or large-scale projects, a detailed assessment fee may apply.

Are your prices fixed or hourly?

It depends on the job. Minor repairs follow a fixed-rate pricing model, while larger or unpredictable tasks may be billed hourly.

Do you provide written quotations?

Absolutely. Every project comes with a detailed written quote outlining scope, materials, and total cost before we begin.

Are there any additional charges I should know about?

Only if unexpected issues arise during the repair. We always discuss any additional cost with you before proceeding.

Services & Pricing & Estimates

How can I book an appointment?

You can schedule directly through our website, call us, or message our support team. Booking takes less than a minute.

How soon can I get an appointment?

Most appointments are available within 24–48 hours. Emergency services can be arranged even sooner depending on availability.

Can I reschedule or cancel my booking?

Yes! You can reschedule or cancel up to 12 hours before your appointment at no extra cost.

Do I need to be home during the repair?

For most tasks, yes. But if we’ve arranged access beforehand, we can complete the work without requiring you to be present.

What if the technician is late?

We respect your time. If delays occur, we notify you immediately and provide an updated arrival window.

Quality & Warranty

Do you offer any warranty on your repairs?

Yes, all our repair work comes with a satisfaction guarantee and a service warranty depending on the type of job.

What if the problem comes back after repair?

Just reach out — we’ll revisit and fix the issue promptly under our warranty terms at no additional charge.

Are your technicians certified?

Absolutely. Our team consists of trained, background-checked, and experienced repair professionals.

What materials do you use for repairs?

We only use high-quality, trusted materials to ensure durability, safety, and long-lasting results.

How do you ensure quality on every job?

Our technicians follow a strict checklist, provide transparent updates, and re-inspect their work before completing the service.
